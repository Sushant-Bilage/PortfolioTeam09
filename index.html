Smart City Project Ideas

Idea 1: Structured Sports Data Management System

Efficient management of sports data using graphs, heaps, hashing, and shortest-path algorithms for scheduling and ranking.

Idea 2: Energy-Optimized Mobility Classification System

Optimizes city transportation using greedy strategies, dynamic programming, priority queues, and route-finding algorithms.

Idea 3: Accessible Animated Learning Management System

Inclusive education platform using trees, AVL, graphs, queues, hashing, and sorting algorithms.

Idea 4: Intelligent Waste Classification & Recycling System

Waste management optimization using graphs, BFS, Kruskal, priority queues, and sorting techniques.

Idea 5: Intelligent Water Flow & Leak Management System

Smart water distribution using graph algorithms, Prim, Bellman-Ford, Floyd-Warshall, and priority queues.

Final Conclusion – Smart City Outcomes

Consolidated insights from all five smart city systems, highlighting real-world impact, algorithmic efficiency, and sustainable urban development.
