Intelligent Water Flow & Leak Management System

The Intelligent Water Flow & Leak Management System manages urban water pipelines to minimize leakage, reduce installation cost, and ensure efficient distribution.

Design and Analysis of Algorithms (DAA) concepts are used to model pipeline networks, optimize layout cost, detect leaks, and manage emergency water supply efficiently.
