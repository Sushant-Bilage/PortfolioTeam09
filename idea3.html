Accessible Animated Learning Management System (A-LMS)

The Accessible Animated Learning Management System (A-LMS) is designed to support inclusive education by combining animated lessons, audio narration, and structured learning paths. It is especially useful for children and visually impaired learners who require guided and adaptive educational content.

Design and Analysis of Algorithms (DAA) plays a critical role in organizing learning materials, managing user data efficiently, personalizing learning paths, and tracking student progress in real time.
