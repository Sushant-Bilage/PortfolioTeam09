Accessible Animated Learning Management System (A-LMS)

The Accessible Animated Learning Management System (A-LMS) is designed to support inclusive education by providing animated lessons, audio narration, and structured digital content. It addresses learning challenges faced by children and visually impaired students.

Design and Analysis of Algorithms (DAA) plays a key role in organizing educational data, optimizing learning paths, managing user accessibility preferences, and tracking progress efficiently in real-time systems.
