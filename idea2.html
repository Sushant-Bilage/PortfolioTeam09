Energy-Optimized Mobility Classification System

Modern smart cities rely heavily on electric transportation, including E-buses, E-autos, metro feeders, shared EV bikes, and bicycles. The challenge is to identify transport modes that minimize energy consumption while keeping travel cost efficient.

This system applies Design and Analysis of Algorithms (DAA) concepts to classify city transport, calculate minimum fare based on distance, display public transport timings, and suggest energy-efficient mobility options.
