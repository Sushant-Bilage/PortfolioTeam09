The Structured Sports Data Management System is designed to manage all sports-related activities in a smart city‚Äîplayer profiles, match schedules, performance tracking, rankings, and tournament progression.

As people grow busier with time, regular sports participation often declines due to lack of coordination and information. This system fills that gap by enabling organized sports activities, identifying nearby players with shared interests, assisting in venue selection, and structuring matches.

The solution is built using core Design and Analysis of Algorithms (DAA) concepts to ensure efficient data handling, scalability, and real-world applicability.
